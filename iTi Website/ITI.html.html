About Us

Why Choose Tech Impart?

Tech Impart (iTi) is the technology and digital skills arm of AfRipul Group — a mission-driven organization committed to driving sustainable growth and development across Africa.

Inspired by the idea of creating a ripple effect across the continent, AfRipul was founded to transform systems, empower people, and reshape Africa’s global narrative. At Tech Impart, we bring that vision to life through practical, industry-focused tech education.

Our goal is simple: equip individuals with real-world digital skills that open doors to opportunities, careers, and innovation — starting from Lomé and expanding across Africa.

  • Backed by the vision and mission of AfRipul Group
  • Practical, project-based training built for real careers
  • Focused on empowering Africa through technology
  • Mentorship, career guidance & real-world exposure
  • Building a new generation of African tech professionals
